Macaroni cheese

Home made Macaroni cheese

Every thing you ever wanted to know about this tasty dish.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups grated cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes.
  2. melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth, about 5 minutes.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour.
  5. Pour in milk slowly, while stirring continuously. Continue to cook and stir until mixture is smooth and bubbling, about 5 minutes, making sure the milk doesn't burn.
  6. Add Cheddar cheese and stir until melted, 2 to 4 minutes.
  7. Drain macaroni and fold into cheese sauce until coated.
  8. Eat
